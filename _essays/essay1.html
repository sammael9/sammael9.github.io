--- title: "A bit of coin" --- Cryptocurrencies are becoming more widely spread every day and blockchain is posing a real threat to the current banking system. The lack of trust and confidence in banks and the overly-complicated economy, as well as the system of surveillance capitalism are forcing people to seek new means of money exchange, as well as different currency models. And sometimes it is purely out of distrust to the system that people are capable of producing something like the cryptocurrencies are.
